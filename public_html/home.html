About Us

What is an escape room?

You might not have played an in-person escape room before but you might have heard of exit rooms, puzzle rooms, or had the chance to play an old-school (or very modern) escape room in a box or a video game.

They need no prior or outside information, as you and your team head into a themed room and race the clock (or friends in adjacent rooms) to triumph over codes, clues, ciphers and other puzles hidden around the room to win.

From family-friendly, to anything but, escape rooms run the gauntlet in themes and tone. They're the perfect activity for groups of friends or family, a date night out, corporate team bonding or a party with a difference... Not sure which one's right for you? Whether it's your first escape room or your fiftieth, contact our helpful team and they'll help get you matchd up to the room for your crew.

Why Lost Locks?

Lost Locks is a locally owned and operated escape room, staffed by long term enthusiasts who want to see everyone else get the chance to play more escape rooms with the new stories, themes, and twists and takes on puzzles for everyone from the first-timer to the old-hand.
